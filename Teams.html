Integrated Product Teams (IPT)

Avionics IPT

The Avionics team handles the design of electronics to acquire live data/video from launch all the way to our project goal of 62+ miles in altitude.

Propulsion IPT

The ACPC Motor team is building a scaled version of our test Ammonium perchlorate composite propellant (APCP) rocket to acheive our 62+ mile altitude goal.

Space Launch Vehicle (SLV) IPT

The SLV team is tasked with building the rocket body itself: the the structure that will house the avionics package and the APCP motor.

Balloon IPT

The Balloon team is creating a lightweight, but durable, structure that will hoise the rocket and its launchpad to 100,000 feet.
